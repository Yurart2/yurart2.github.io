Yur Art   great art for large walls  
   


Kinzie St. Bridge Chicago

  


Large paintings are the "patriarchs" of the more frequent large walls in new homes and loft residences. Great art provides proportional, upscale decorating and creates a presence of art beyond the bounds of past experiences. Like a critic's narrative, I usually write a brief story for my large works to add interest for the owner and provide more information for introspective viewing. All the original oil paintings shown here are 7 ft. x 5 ft. or 5 ft. x 7 ft. oil on canvas.

Contact Donn Ziebell if you have an interest in a fine art 28 in. x 40 in. digital print reproduction.
